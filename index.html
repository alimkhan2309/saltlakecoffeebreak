Our Story

At Salt Lake Coffee Break, we believe that coffee is more than just a drink—it’s an experience. Located near trolly station, our cafe is dedicated to bringing you the finest blends and a warm, inviting atmosphere. Our baristas are passionate about every cup they serve, using only the highest quality beans and traditional techniques to create your favorite beverages.
